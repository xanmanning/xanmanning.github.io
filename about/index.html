Profile

Linux fanatic, creative type, geek.

During the course of my university education in architecture I made use of verbal and visual communication techniques to varying audiences in both individual and team work situations. I strive towards a satisfied completion of projects but take time to learn and reflect upon the processes undertaken.

In my current role I am a Linux Systems Administrator, provisioning and maintaining web hosting infrastructure on Amazon Web Services tailored to run Drupal. The key focuses of the job is to deliver stable, scalable and reliable hosting that integrates a wide range of Amazon’s services to give the customer the best experience. Alongside day-to-day duties I am required to test and document new features and technologies that might enhance a project’s performance or functionality.

In my previous role I provided SQL report writing services to our Project Implementation team. Our products use FastReports and Microsoft SQL Server Reporting Services (SSRS) for generating reports. In addition, my role included writing SQL scripts for interfacing our software with other solutions. When I was not providing support to our Implementation team, I developed the company’s Web Dashboard product written in C# and ASP.NET and the Licensing record system written in PHP. The lateral problem solving nature of architecture and the logical mentality of coding has aided my progression in the field Information Technology.

In brief, I am an eager, self-motivated and multi-disciplined individual always striving to learn and genuinely interested in design processes from the inception to the detailing and the completion.
