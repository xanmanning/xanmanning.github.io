About

Profile

An eager, self-motivated and multi-disciplined individual; the majority of my professional experience has been providing Operational support, but with a focus on cloud technologies and automation. I strive to work smarter.

In my current role I am a Technical Support Analyst working within Operations and Engineering. Day-to-day I provide operational support for customers; predominantly on SAP HANA appliances which run Enterprise Linux distributions but also for customers running a multitude of applications on AIX and VMWare. The primary focus of operations documentation is to formalise processes to ultimately provide a basis for automation.

My Engineering workload focuses on providing support and ability around public cloud providers such as Amazon Web Services and Microsoft Azure. The work requires repeatability and automation to be considered so I am often exposed to “Infrastructure as Code” practices with Configuration management tools such as Ansible and Terraform.

In my previous role as a DevOps Engineer, my primary epic was to migrate customers from Rackspace to AWS and to reduce the operations workload. Goals included improving monitoring by implementing Nagios, creating a framework configuration for moving from CFEngine3 to SaltStack and develop tools for automating deployment of projects into AWS based on the existing tools built for Rackspace Cloud.

During the course of my university education in architecture I made use of verbal and visual communication techniques to varying audiences in both individual and team work situations. I strive towards a satisfied completion of projects but take time to learn and reflect upon the processes undertaken.
