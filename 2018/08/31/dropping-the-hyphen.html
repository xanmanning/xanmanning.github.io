Dropping the Hyphen!

Aug 31, 2018
1 min read

You may think I’m mad, but I am going to drop the hyphen in my domain, so very shortly you will see that instead of visiting https://xan-manning.co.uk, you will be visiting https://xanmanning.co.uk.

Why are you doing this, Xan?

Well, you have probably noticed that pretty much every other vanity domain on the net is lacking a hyphen between firstname and surname; I’m just following that convention.

I’ll keep the ol’ xan-manning.co.uk domain, but it will redirect to xanmanning.co.uk.

Sorry folks.
